Help and frequently asked questions

How to register?

In the menu find a button called Sign In. When you click on it, a menu drops and in the bottom you can see a link called Register.

How to log in to my account?

In the menu find a button called Sign In. When you click on it, a menu drops and there are the fields for username and password which you have to fill.

How to edit my profile?

In the menu click on your name. A menu drops and you see an option My profile. Click on it and you go to your profile, where you can see a button Account Settings.

How to upload a video?

In the menu there is a button called Upload video. Enter the fields and click on Upload.

How to delete a video?

In the menu above click on your name. A menu drops and you see an option My videos. This is the place where you can manage your videos.

How to browse videos?

Go to the home page and click Discover videos.

How to find a particular video?

Use the search field which you can find in the menu above.

Congratulations!

Your registration is completed!

You can now log into your account or go back to the homepage.
